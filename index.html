Privacy Policy for SnapPrivate

Last updated: July 23, 2026

SnapPrivate is a privacy-first browser extension for capturing and annotating screenshots.

Data Collection

We collect no personal data.

Screenshots and annotations are processed entirely on your device. They are never uploaded to any server controlled by us.

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome’s local storage only to remember:

  • Daily capture count (to enforce the free plan limit of 5 captures per day)
  • License / Pro status (if you upgrade)

This data stays on your computer and is not transmitted to us.

Permissions

  • activeTab / scripting — required to capture the current page
  • storage — required for local settings and limits

We do not read your browsing history or page content beyond what is needed for the screenshot you request.

Third Parties

If you choose to upgrade to Pro, payment is processed by a third-party payment provider (ExtensionPay or similar). Their privacy policy applies to payment data.

We do not use analytics services that track your screenshots or visited pages.

Children’s Privacy

SnapPrivate is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change accordingly.

Contact

Questions about privacy: contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support form for this extension.
